Fries

Description

The trick to restaurant-style sweet potatoes fries is to not cut them too thin and to not over-fill the basket when cooking.

Ingredients

  • 600g sweet potatoes, about 3 small, peeled
  • 20g cornflour
  • half tsp onion granules
  • half tsp garlic granules
  • half tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Recipe

  1. Preheat the air-fryer to 200C.
  2. Cut the sweet potatoes into chips no smaller than 1cm thick.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the cornflour, onion and garlic granules, paprika and plenty of salt and black pepper. Add the sweet potato and toss until well coated.
  4. Tip out any excess cornflour before pouring over the oil, ensuring all the chips are coated.
  5. Add the sweet potato fries to the air-fryer in an even layer. Cook for 25-30 mins, turning the chips over after 20mins.
  6. When the sweet potato fries are well browned and tender, carefully lift out into a serving bowl. The fries will crisp up further as they cool slightly.
